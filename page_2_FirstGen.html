First Gen - Supporting First Generation Students

About First Gen

The First Gen program at the University of Michigan is dedicated to providing resources, support, and community for students who are the first in their families to attend college. This initiative aims to ensure a welcoming and empowering experience for all first-generation students.

Key Programs and Support

Mentorship Opportunities

First Gen provides mentorship programs where students are paired with faculty, staff, or peers who offer guidance, support, and encouragement throughout their academic journey.

Workshops and Events

Engage in workshops, seminars, and events designed to address the unique challenges and opportunities faced by first-generation students.

Community Connections

Build lasting friendships and professional connections through a supportive network of first-generation students and alumni.
