Student Life - Basic Needs

About Basic Needs

The Basic Needs program at the University of Michigan is committed to ensuring that every student has access to the essential resources they need to succeed, including food, housing, and financial assistance. This initiative works to eliminate barriers so students can focus on their education and well-being.

Key Services

Food Assistance

Access food pantries, meal programs, and emergency food assistance to ensure no student goes hungry.

Housing Support

Receive help with finding affordable housing, managing rent, and addressing housing instability.

Financial Assistance

Connect with financial aid resources, emergency funding, and budgeting workshops to manage expenses effectively.
